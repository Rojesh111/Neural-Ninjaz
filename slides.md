# 🛡️ BureacyBuster: Zero-Trust Document Intelligence
### High-Stakes Legal Ingestion & Secure Retrieval

---

## 🚀 Introduction
**BureacyBuster** is an advanced AI platform designed to transform chaotic bureaucratic workflows into structured, secure intelligence. 

### 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: React 18 (Vite), Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, Lucide Icons.
- **Backend**: FastAPI (Python), MongoDB (ODMantic), Uvicorn.
- **AI Engine**: Azure OpenAI (GPT-4o Vision & Text).
- **Security**: Local ML (Scikit-Learn) + Guard AI Audit.

---

## 🚩 The Problem Statement
Modern bureaucracy suffers from three critical failures when adopting AI:

1. **The Accuracy Gap**: Vector databases often fragment legal context, leading to hallucinations.
2. **The Data Leak**: Sensitive identity documents (Passports, ID cards) are often stored without proper "Consent Handshakes."
3. **Adversarial Attacks**: Prompt injection roleplay can easily bypass standard system prompts to leak private data.

---

## 🧠 How BureacyBuster Solves It
We bridge the gap between **Absolute Privacy** and **Absolute Intelligence**.

- **Vectorless Intelligence**: Instead of vectors, we use a structured **JSON Map** for 100% accurate legal retrieval.
- **Zero-Trust Handshake**: The AI is physically locked out of personal data until it receives explicit linguistic consent.
- **Hybrid Security**: A dual-layer firewall that kills malicious intent before it reaches the core LLM.

---

## 👁️ OCR and Page Indexing
### Beyond simple text extraction.

1. **Vision-Powered Analysis**: Uses GPT-4o Vision to "see" document layout, preserving the hierarchy of Headings, Subheadings, and Paragraphs.
2. **Interactive Handover**: Users can reorder pages manually before indexing to ensure logical consistency.
3. **Real-Time Tracking**: Full-page progress tracking with a live "Transcription Loop" directly from the Vision API.

---

## 🛡️ Security & Prompt Injection
### Our Two-Layer Defense Architecture

- **Layer 1: Sentinel ML**: A high-speed, local Random Forest classifier that identifies adversarial patterns in milliseconds.
- **Layer 2: Guard AI Audit**: Suspicious queries are sent to a specialized Security Agent for a deep linguistic audit of intent.
- **Result**: Definitive protection against system prompt bypass, roleplay attacks, and unauthorized data extraction.
